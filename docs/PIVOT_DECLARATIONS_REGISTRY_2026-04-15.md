## Operating Rules

- New pivots dock to existing surfaces first: continuity, blackboard, theater, reports, help, route reports, and existing workbench/runtime contracts.
- Completion retracts downward one layer. Do not leave the system stranded at a higher declaration after the blocker is cleared.
- No pivot creates a second truth plane.
- Pan-facing pivots must consume existing support/contact truth:
  - `support_polygon`
  - `support_polygon_world`
  - `contact_patch`
  - `support_y`
  - route/support reports
  - `workbench_stage_contact`
- Associative surfaces should carry pivots forward so a compressed restart can recover the current top declaration without transcript archaeology.
