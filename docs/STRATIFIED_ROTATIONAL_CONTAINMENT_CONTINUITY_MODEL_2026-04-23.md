# Stratified Rotational Containment Continuity Model 2026-04-23

Repo: `D:\End-Game\champion_councl`

Status: active continuity doctrine

## Purpose

Capture the operator-provided spatial model for continuity:

- hot core / furnace
- onion-like safety strata
- upper clean occupancy
- lower service / recovery bands
- controlled transfer floors
- Rubik-style bounded rotation

This is not a new authority plane.

It is a spatial intuition for how continuity should remain stable, safe, and manipulable without flattening everything into one vague memory field.

## Bottom Line

Continuity should be understood as a layered containment and transfer system:

- live truth stays at the center
- readable live occupancy stays above
- archive, planning, stale, and unresolved material stay in bounded service bands
- transfers between those bands happen through explicit gates
- one bounded face can be foregrounded or rotated without collapsing the whole stack

Short name:

- `stratified_rotational_containment`

Working image:

- onion-wrapped reactor with Rubik-style packet rotation

## Core Mapping

### 1. Furnace / Core

This is the hottest truth-pressure source:

- live runtime truth
- current active seam
- current contradiction pressure
- current operator objective

This is not the docs lane, not archive continuity, and not Dreamer speculation.

### 2. Upper Clean Occupancy

These are the surfaces the operator should be able to stand on safely:

- live theater / text-theater read
- browser-visible corroboration when needed
- `blackboard.working_set.query_thread`
- `output_state`

These are the readable occupancy lanes.
They should stay stable while lower bands do service work.

### 3. Lower Service / Recovery Bands

These are the bounded service layers:

- `continuity_packet`
- `docs_packet`
- archive posture
- stale or lagged carried state
- unresolved doctrinal residue
- misunderstanding boxes

These layers may carry useful material, but they do not get to masquerade as live truth.

### 4. Transfer Floors / Service Lifts

These are the controlled movement lanes between bands:

- `continuity_restore`
- paired-state comparison / alignment
- `trajectory_correlator`
- `continuity_cue`
- scoped `env_report(...)`

Their job is to move pressure, material, and orientation safely between bands.

### 5. Scout / Measurement / Proposal Lanes

These are not truth by themselves, but they help route inquiry:

- `Tinkerbell` points
- `Pan` measures
- `Dreamer` proposes and scores
- `field_disposition` explains the active medium

They should help decide what to inspect or compare next.
They should not silently promote themselves into authority.

## Rubik Rule

Rubik belongs here as the bounded permutation model:

- one face rotates into view
- one packet can be foregrounded
- one transfer can happen without rewriting the whole cube
- a local reorientation does not collapse the surrounding structure

Rubik is not the whole ontology.

It is the manipulation grammar over the larger stratified system.

## Onion Rule

Onion belongs here as the safety-shell model:

- hotter truth pressure at the center
- outer shells regulate access
- stale, dirty, or unresolved material stays in the correct band
- the operator does not have to stand directly on the service layer to benefit from it

Safety feeling comes from bounded strata, not from denial that the lower layers exist.

## Weight / Counterweight Rule

The gravity / counterweight analogy is useful, but only in the right way.

It means:

- leverage
- stored potential
- controlled return force
- guided transfer
- bounded suspension of an unresolved load

It does not mean:

- free energy
- self-powering truth
- authority without corroboration

## Misunderstanding Box As Suspended Load

When a misunderstanding is load-bearing:

- isolate it as a bounded suspended box
- keep verified footing stable outside it
- let it raise pressure through the orienting surfaces
- route the next comparison, teaching read, or corroboration toward it
- only return it upward when it has been cleaned by real understanding

This is the continuity equivalent of routing dirty furniture to a lower service floor while keeping the upper room clean and usable.

## Operator Experience

If the upper room is working correctly:

- the system feels safe
- the live room stays habitable
- confusion does not smear everywhere
- unresolved material has somewhere to go
- re-entry feels deliberate rather than panicked

That feeling is not fake.

It is the perceptual value created by a real service architecture below the visible room.

## Guardrails

1. Live truth remains primary.
2. Service bands support occupancy; they do not replace truth.
3. Archive and planning packets may recommend, but they do not decide live reality.
4. Dreamer may suggest next reads or candidate mappings, but it does not get to hallucinate a clean closure.
5. Rotation/permutation is for bounded reorientation, not arbitrary truth mutation.
6. Safety strata should make the system more stable, not more occult.

## Recovery Use

When continuity is lost or a hot misunderstanding appears:

1. identify the current hot core
2. identify which room or band the material belongs in
3. keep upper-room footing anchored on verified live surfaces
4. route dirty, stale, or unresolved material into a bounded service band
5. use the transfer floor to decide the next corroboration or teaching move
6. only reintroduce cleaned material into the upper room when the release condition is met
