`brotology` is the field language for operators mechanizing their way through dynamic domains of expertise.

Its practical branches now read like this:

- `science surfing`
- `architect surfology`
- `field utility`

- no fake sovereignty over the actual substrate

## Primary Branches

### 1. Science Surfing

Science surfers are field operators who move through dynamic conditions rather than pretending the field is static.

Core posture:

- measure while moving
- ride pressure instead of denying it
- use local reads to stay alive and useful
- learn the field by contact, timing, rebound, and return path

Current runtime docking surfaces:

- `pan_probe`
- `balance`
- contact/support truth
- `range_gate`
- `reach_envelope`
- capture surfaces

### 2. Architect Surfology

Architect surfology is the structural and sequencing branch.

This is where operators:

- mechanize the route
- align the envelopes
- stage the sequence
- coordinate acceleration without breaking truth

Current runtime docking surfaces:

- `query_thread`
- `output_state`
- `trajectory_correlator`
- `sequence_field.force_wave`
- authored pose / timeline / clip lanes

### 3. Field Utility

Field utility is the cargo-bay logic:

- what carries the load
- what carries the tools
- what is decorative
- what is load-bearing

Current runtime docking surfaces:

- truth spine
- `output_state`
- `docs_packet`
- `continuity_packet`
- `misunderstanding_box`
- `watch_board`
- captures and reports

### 4. Dark-Field Caution Lanes

These are hazardous specialization zones where a lane can become distorted or hoard authority.

Current named caution lane:

- `brotato landscaping`

Current meaning:

- accumulation without circulation
- draconian hoarding
- overgrowth of side surfaces
- utility or decoration trying to become sovereign truth

This is a caution label, not a promoted runtime subsystem.

### 5. Derived Fringe Fields

These are not primary runtime contracts.

They are field-speciation overlays that emerged from actual operation, sequencing, and comparative observation.

Current named fringe fields:

- `misty teek`
- `code orangutang`
- `brolativity`
- `bromanticism`
- `mullet life`
- `brohandoism`

These remain subordinate to canon surfaces and should be treated as:

- operator-language overlays
- style/posture taxonomies
- continuity-carried explanation layers

Not:

- replacement contracts
- secret controllers
- sovereign architecture names

## Field Chronology / Speciation

The current Brotology chronology now reads like this:

1. `science surfing`
   Local contact, field pressure, practical movement, real reads.

2. `architect surfology`
   Sequencing, route logic, admissibility, force carriage, structural alignment.

3. `field utility`
   Load-bearing vs decorative distinction, cargo-bay reasoning, tool carriage.

4. `dark-field caution lanes`
   Places where accumulation, distortion, or side-surface sovereignty start to creep in.

5. `misty teek`
   A fringe field at the edge of the Vitruvian / shuttering / force-wave lane where the operator uses literal-first contact with the whole visible range before abstracting. It is part fatherly composure, part “look in charge while figuring it out,” and part referential dance grammar.

6. `code orangutang`
   The evasive servo/controller fringe: dodge most of the time, soften the blow at impact, keep the egg intact, and redirect force through bounded contact rather than taking it square.

7. `brolativity`
   The operator contribution that frames all of the above as a referential posture: classy up top, bunker planning below, keeping the front readable while the back-stage reasoning remains disciplined and active.

8. `bromanticism`
   The mythic and sentimental charge layer: loyalty, wonder, memorial humor, meadow-scale sincerity, and the real feeling under the joke.

9. `mullet life`
   The personal/personified expression of that split:
   public composure up front, planning trench in the back.

10. `brohandoism`
    The musical bridge fringe. The operator uses musical register as a cross-cultural interfacing protocol. Sound and rhythm carry where text fails. Bridging, not crossing — the origin culture is never burned for the destination. Sibling to `dance_bridge` and `hair_bridge`; together they form the three-sibling bridge family.

This chronology is descriptive, not exclusive.

It is closer to martial-art field speciation than to a rigid taxonomy.

- one truth spine
- many bounded utility surfaces

- rename layers are for coordination, memory, and explanation

- if a Brotology label and a canonical runtime name conflict, the canonical runtime name wins
