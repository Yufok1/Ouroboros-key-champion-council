## Purpose

Capture the compound facility idea in one place:

- `brohando` = musical bridge
- `sky` = aerial or orbital vantage
- `giving` = non-extractive public-value law
- `facility` = actual installation, not pure vibe

## Bottom Line

`brohandoskygivingfacility` is the facility-form of the current vessel lane.

Peacetime read:

- value broadcaster
- cultural bridge
- utility platform
- public-facing signal tower with honest service architecture underneath

Peril read:

- the same facility becomes a node in the Civic Protection Lattice

## Why The Musical Bridge Matters

The `brohando` part is not decoration.

It means the facility has a cultural interface that can cross unfamiliar groups without requiring prior shared jargon.

That gives the facility:

- a human invitation surface
- a cross-cultural signal path
- a non-bureaucratic way to reach people before and during stress

## Why The Sky Vantage Matters

The `sky` part is not only altitude.

It means:

- broad line of sight
- relay position
- observation advantage
- symbolic public readability

## Diagnostic Minimum

A serious drawing of this facility must show:

1. top signal / broadcast surfaces
2. operator-vessel lane
3. service core
4. clean / dirty split
5. public receivers
6. peril-mode translation path

## Guardrails

1. The facility is not allowed to claim "giving" without receivers.
2. The facility is not allowed to claim "sky" while hiding the service core.
3. The facility is not allowed to claim culture while severing utility.
4. The facility must remain a facility, not just a mascot.
