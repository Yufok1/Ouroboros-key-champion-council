## Guardrails

The reviewing model must not:

- smuggle in perpetual-motion language as if it were solved
- collapse the public demonstrator and the megastructure into one step
- confuse the cultural/Brotology layer with the engineering claim
- invent a second hidden controller reality when one truth spine is already declared
- recommend vague "further research" without naming a concrete next artifact

The reviewing model should:

- separate trigger, charge, throw, catch, return, and recharge
- separate visible panel behavior from structural truth
- separate service-band utility from upper-band perception value
- be specific about risks, contradictions, and missing data
